1 : Most of the examples so far have shown regular
2 : expressions applied to static strings.
3 : The following example shows one way to apply regular
4 : expressions to search for matches in a file.
5 : Inspired by Unix’s grep, JGrep.java takes two arguments: 
6 : a filename and the regular expression that you want to match.
7 : The output shows each line where a match occurs and the
8 :  match position(s) within the line.
